NEW POONA BAKERY

New Poona Bakery is a heritage brand that serves a wide range sweet and savory delicacies. Right from fresh wholesome bread to rich velvety cakes, distinctively flaky cream rolls, buttery cookies and our star product – the Vegetable pattice; the list is endless! What started as a single outlet in Pune city, has now spread to 100+ outlets in and around Pune.
